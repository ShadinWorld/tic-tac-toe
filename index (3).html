Tic-Tac-Toe

About this app

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player strategy game played on a 3×3 grid. Take turns placing your mark and try to line up three in a row before your opponent does.

How to play

  • Player X always goes first, then players alternate turns.
  • Click any empty square to place your mark there.
  • Get 3 marks in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — to win.
  • If all 9 squares fill up with no winner, the game is a draw.
  • New Game clears the board and keeps the score.
  • Reset Scores sets the scoreboard back to zero.
